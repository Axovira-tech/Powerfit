Our Story

Building Stronger Communities Since 2014

Welcome to POWERFIT, the premier destination for fitness enthusiasts. Founded in 2014, we set out with a simple mission: to create a gym that felt like a second home. A place where you are not just a member number, but part of a thriving community.

Starting with a small warehouse and a passion for iron, we have grown into a state-of-the-art facility featuring 30,000 sq ft of workout space, top-tier equipment, and a team of world-class trainers dedicated to your success.
